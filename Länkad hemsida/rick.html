Rick


Richard "Rick" Sanchez of Dimension C-137, commonly referred to as Rick C-137,
is the titular main protagonist of the Rick and Morty franchise.
He is a megagenius[5] scientist whose alcoholism and reckless,
nihilistic behavior are sources of concern for his "daughter's" family,
as well as the safety of their son, Morty. Upon the series' inception,
Rick's mysterious origins and mental health were large sources of speculation.
As time has gone on, more has been revealed about his character;
his backstory explaining much of his behavior.
At the same time, Rick has continuously committed more heinous acts[6][7],
certifying him as the series' anti-hero, bordering on antagonist.
