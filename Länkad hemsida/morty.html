Morty


Mortimer Chauncey "Morty" Smith Sr. of the Prime Universe/Cronenberged Dimension
is the titular deuteragonist of Rick and Morty. He is also one of the five main characters of Rick and Morty.
He is the grandson of Rick Sanchez and is often forced to tag along on his various misadventures.
Morty attends Harry Herpson High School along with his sister, Summer.[8]
At the beginning of the series, Morty was shown to be an incredibly anxious and submissive teenage boy.
However, after going on many so-called adventures with Rick, Morty has seemingly become more confident
and his own person[9], independent from Rick, rather than a mere sidekick.
